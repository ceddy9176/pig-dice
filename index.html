Rules!

First Player to 100 pts wins!

  • Player 1 goes first
  • Click the "Roll" button to roll a virtual 6-sided die
  • To end turn, select "End Turn" button- this will add the "Roll Total" to the player's score
  • If you roll a 1, your turn is over and you get NO POINTS
  • Have fun! :D
